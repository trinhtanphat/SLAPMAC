# SlapMac Extension Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-01

SlapMac is a local-only browser extension.

## What SlapMac Accesses

- Microphone input, only when you choose Microphone mode and grant permission
- Motion sensor data, only when you choose Motion Sensor mode and your browser/device supports it
- Local extension storage, to save your settings such as enabled state, sensitivity, volume, cooldown, detection mode, and slap count

## What SlapMac Does Not Do

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not send audio, motion, or usage data to any server
- Does not use analytics, ads, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts
- Does not make external network requests
- Does not sell or share your data

## How Data Is Stored

All settings are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome extension storage API.
No cloud sync or remote database is used by the extension itself.

## Permissions

SlapMac requests the minimum permission set needed for operation:

- `storage`: saves your local settings and slap counter
- Microphone permission: requested by the browser only when you enable microphone-based detection

## Data Retention

Your settings remain in local browser storage until you clear extension data, remove the extension, or reset the browser profile.
